About Us

In September 2021, we decided to launch FoodHub after a realizing how delicious restaurant made food, worth hundreds of dollars, was out of reach of many people not only because of money constraints, but also because of the nobody ever thought that restaurant could serve people just by sharing their recipes, just for people to try them home at times when they want to spend a family cooking night or simply more time home. FoodHub was made with one aim to bring chef recipes to people straight from the restaurants that got their customers back.
